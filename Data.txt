The data is about an Ecommerce Customers from a company where they can buy products from either on a mobile app or website. The dataset has Customer info, suchas Email, Address, and their color Avatar. Then it also has numerical value columns:

* Avg. Session Length: Average session of in-store style advice sessions.
* Time on App: Average time spent on App in minutes
* Time on Website: Average time spent on Website in minutes
* Length of Membership: How many years the customer has been a member. 

Goal is to decide whether to focus efforts on their mobile app experience or their website.
